Developed by: Hristo Ivanov
Asset Store Publisher: Kris Development

HOW TO USE:
Navigate to "Kris Development > Favorite Scenes".
Dock the window somewhere you see fit.
Click on the drag a scene asset to the scene field or press "Pin Current Scene" to add a scene to the list.
Press the scene from the list to instantly load it in the editor.
"O" to locate your scene asset in the project.
"Add" allows you to additively load the scene.
"X" will remove the scene from the pinned scenes list.
